BMW is a German multinational company that produces cars and motorcycles. The company was founded in 1916 as a manufacturer of aircraft engines, which produced from 1917 to 1918 and again from 1933 to 1945. Cars are marketed under the BMW, Mini and Rolls-Royce brands, and motorcycles are marketed under the BMW Motorrad brand. It is one of the list of competitors in cars in the world and is the biggest competitor to other very popular German cars such as Mercedes, Audi, Volkswagen, Maybach and Smart. In 2015, BMW was the second largest car producer in the world, with 2,279,503 vehicles produced.
