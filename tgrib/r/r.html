Article on Rolls-Royce Design

The Phantom is distinguished by classic English-style design in addition to the tremendous engineering innovations added to it by BMW, we find that the car features a long rear hood, a long wheelbase and a short front cover. As for the wheels, they are made of aluminum with a diameter of up to 21 inches, and they are also equipped with airless running technology for a distance of up to 160 km, and in the front there are headlights made of xenon with a vision detector through the fog, which is characterized by its circular shape. The famous Rolls-Royce in the form of an angel with wings sculpted specifically for the company and equipped with high technology as it is dynamic; Where it can be hidden or shown, it is also equipped with a safety technology, so it enters its ambush at the moment of impact directly.


Rolls-Royce car engine

The car's engine is a new 6.75-liter V-12 with two turbochargers. This engine produces 563 hp, which is up from the 453 hp naturally produced by the V-12 engine in the previous Rolls Phantom. Torque is increased to 664 lb-ft, instead of 531 lb-ft in the old model. Power is directed through an eight-speed ZF automatic transmission. Rolls sprints from rest to 62 mph in 5.3 seconds on the short-wheeled model and 5.4 seconds on the longer model, and weighs 5,948 pounds net without payload.


Rolls-Royce car features

It comes with a luxurious cabin filled with genuine leather and wood trim and a rear cabin that seems to rise above the clouds thanks to a suspension system that is perhaps the most comfortable in all the world and that monitors the road condition to adapt to it in advance, and mechanically it comes with a 563-horsepower V12 engine, with the ability to accelerate from 0 to 100 km / h in 5.4 seconds.
